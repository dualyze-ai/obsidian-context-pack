# Museum Guide

A guide to seeing the [[Masterpieces of the World]] in person. Reproductions are no substitute — brushwork, scale, and color only reveal themselves in front of the real thing.

## Essential Museums

### Europe
| Museum | City | Must-See |
|---|---|---|
| Musée du Louvre | Paris | Mona Lisa, Venus de Milo |
| Musée d'Orsay | Paris | Impressionist collection |
| Rijksmuseum | Amsterdam | The Night Watch |
| Mauritshuis | The Hague | Girl with a Pearl Earring |
| Uffizi Gallery | Florence | Botticelli, Renaissance masters |
| Vatican Museums | Vatican City | Sistine Chapel |

### North America
| Museum | City | Must-See |
|---|---|---|
| Metropolitan Museum of Art | New York | Vast collection, all eras |
| MoMA | New York | Starry Night, Les Demoiselles |
| Art Institute of Chicago | Chicago | Seurat, Monet, Picasso |

## Tips for Visiting

- Pre-book timed entry tickets for the Louvre and Vatican — queues are brutal without them
- Arrive at opening or in the final hour before closing for calmer viewing
- Audio guides at major museums are well worth the small fee
