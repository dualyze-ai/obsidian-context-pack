# Art for Beginners

Starting with the [[Masterpieces of the World]] is the best on-ramp to art appreciation. No expertise required — just curiosity.

## How to Look at a Painting

1. **Start with your gut** — Do you like it? Dislike it? Why?
2. **Learn the context** — Knowing the era and the artist's life transforms what you see.
3. **Notice the technique** — Brushwork, color, composition. What is the artist choosing to do?
