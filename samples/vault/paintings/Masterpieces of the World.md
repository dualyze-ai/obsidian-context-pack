# Masterpieces of the World

Exploring the great paintings of human history — the movements, eras, and artists behind them.

## Major Art Movements

- [[Impressionism]] — Late 19th-century France. Capturing light and fleeting moments in bold color.
- [[Renaissance]] — 14th–17th century. Revival of classical ideals. Humanism and perspective.
- [[Baroque]] — 17th century. Dramatic light and shadow, emotional intensity.
- [[Modern Art]] — Late 19th–20th century. Experiments in form, color, and concept.

## Seeing the Masterpieces in Person

Visiting museums around the world reveals brushwork, scale, and color that reproductions can never convey.
