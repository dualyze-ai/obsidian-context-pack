# Renaissance

A cultural and artistic rebirth that swept Europe from the 14th to 17th centuries, centered in Italy. Artists rediscovered classical antiquity, pioneered mathematical perspective, and placed humanity at the center of art and thought.

## Key Characteristics

- Mathematical perspective (linear perspective)
- Realistic anatomy based on the study of human bodies
- Mythological and religious themes
- Flourished in Florence, Venice, and Rome

## Leading Painters

- [[Leonardo da Vinci]] — Painted the Mona Lisa and The Last Supper. The ultimate Renaissance man.
- [[Michelangelo]] — Sistine Chapel ceiling. As much sculptor as painter.
- [[Raphael]] — Idealized beauty in Madonnas and the School of Athens.

## Related Movements

- [[Baroque]] — The dramatic, emotionally charged style that followed the Renaissance's classical balance.
