# Modern Art

An umbrella term for the radical experimental movements from the late 19th through the 20th century. Building on Impressionism's break from tradition, Modern Art questioned the very nature of form, color, perspective, and meaning.

## Major Movements

| Movement | Key Ideas |
|---|---|
| Post-Impressionism | Personal emotion, structure, symbolism |
| Cubism | Multiple simultaneous viewpoints |
| Surrealism | Dreams, the unconscious, the irrational |
| Expressionism | Raw emotional intensity over realistic depiction |

## Leading Painters

- [[Vincent van Gogh]] — Swirling brushstrokes and electric color. Starry Night, Sunflowers.
- [[Pablo Picasso]] — Founded Cubism. The most influential artist of the 20th century.
- [[Salvador Dalí]] — Surrealism's most famous face. The Persistence of Memory.

## Related Movements

- [[Impressionism]] — The revolutionary starting point for all of Modern Art.
