# Travel Guide

A destination index covering Japan and international travel spots.

## Japan

### Kansai
- [[Kyoto]] — Ancient imperial capital. UNESCO heritage sites, temples, matcha sweets.
- [[Osaka]] — Japan's kitchen. Dotonbori, takoyaki, USJ.
- [[Nara]] — Free-roaming deer and the Great Buddha. Compact day-trip gem.

### Kanto (Day Trips & Nearby)
- [[Kamakura]] — Great Buddha, seaside temples, and Shonan coast vibes.
- [[Hakone]] — Hot springs with stunning Mt Fuji panoramas.
- [[Nikko]] — UNESCO Toshogu Shrine and Kegon Falls.

### Hokkaido
- [[Hakodate]] — Spectacular night view, morning market, fresh seafood.
- [[Furano]] — Sweeping lavender fields and wide open skies.

### Kyushu
- [[Nagasaki]] — Dejima, Glover Garden, and champon noodles.
- [[Beppu]] — Japan's hot spring capital. Eight "hells" to explore.

## International

### Europe
- [[Paris]] — Eiffel Tower, Louvre, croissants, and world-class wine.
- [[Barcelona]] — Gaudí masterpieces, beach, and tapas culture.
- [[Rome]] — Colosseum, Vatican, and the world's best pasta.

### Asia
- [[Bangkok]] — Temple-hopping, street food paradise, and vibrant nightlife.
- [[Bali]] — Terraced rice paddies, surf breaks, and Hindu temples.
- [[Taipei]] — Night markets, xiaolongbao, and the National Palace Museum.
- [[Seoul]] — K-culture, Korean BBQ, and palaces.

### Others
- [[New York City]] — Statue of Liberty, MoMA, Broadway, and the High Line.
- [[Honolulu (Hawaii)]] — Waikiki Beach, loco moco, and the North Shore.
- [[Sydney]] — Opera House, Bondi Beach, and gateway to the Great Barrier Reef.
