# Recipe Index

A curated index of Japanese, Chinese, and Western home-cooking recipes.

## Japanese

- [[Nikujaga]] — Beef and potato simmered in sweet-savory sauce. The ultimate comfort food.
- [[Tonjiru (Pork Miso Soup)]] — Hearty miso soup loaded with pork and root vegetables.
- [[Karaage (Japanese Fried Chicken)]] — Crispy, juicy marinated fried chicken.
- [[Oyakodon]] — Fluffy chicken and egg rice bowl.
- [[Dashimaki Tamago]] — Dashi-flavored rolled omelette.
- [[Chikuzen-ni]] — Simmered root vegetables and chicken. A New Year's staple.
- [[Grilled Saury (Sanma Shioyaki)]] — Autumn's iconic salted grilled fish.
- [[Kakuni (Braised Pork Belly)]] — Melt-in-your-mouth slow-braised pork.

## Chinese

- [[Mapo Tofu]] — Silky tofu in numbing Sichuan chili sauce.
- [[Dan Dan Noodles (Tantanmen)]] — Sesame and spice noodle soup.
- [[Ebi Chili (Shrimp in Chili Sauce)]] — Plump shrimp in a tangy chili sauce.
- [[Sweet and Sour Pork (Subuta)]] — Classic sweet-and-sour pork stir-fry.
- [[Chahan (Chinese Fried Rice)]] — Light and fluffy wok-fried rice.

## Western

- [[Japanese Hamburg Steak]] — Juicy pan-seared hamburger patty with demi-glace sauce.
- [[Korokke (Japanese Croquettes)]] — Crispy panko-coated potato croquettes.
- [[Beef Stew]] — Rich red-wine braised beef stew.
- [[Minestrone]] — Vegetable-packed Italian tomato soup.
- [[Spaghetti Carbonara]] — Creamy egg-and-cheese pasta.
- [[Macaroni Gratin]] — Bubbling béchamel and cheese bake.
- [[Omurice]] — Fluffy omelette over ketchup fried rice.
