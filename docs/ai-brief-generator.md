AI Brief Generator analyzes your vault and creates a high-level diagnostic report.

Instead of manually exploring dozens or hundreds of notes, AI Brief helps you understand:

- Topic Clusters
- Knowledge Maps
- Relationship Maps
- Knowledge Health
- Development Candidates
- Suggested AI Prompts

---

## What is an AI Brief?

An AI Brief is an automatically generated overview of a folder, project, or knowledge base.

It helps you understand:

- What topics exist
- How they are connected
- Which notes are central
- Which areas can be expanded
- What questions remain unanswered
