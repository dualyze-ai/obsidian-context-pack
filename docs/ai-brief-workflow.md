# AI Brief Workflow

AI Context Pack supports a structured workflow:

```text
Analyze → Organize → Export → Ask
```

## 1. Analyze

Generate an AI Brief from a folder.

AI Brief identifies:

- Topic clusters
- Knowledge maps
- Relationship maps
- Knowledge health
- Suggested prompts

## 2. Visualize

Review the Knowledge Map to understand topic clusters and representative notes.

## 3. Organize

Generate an AI MOC from the AI Brief.

The AI MOC turns analysis into a navigable knowledge structure.

## 4. Export

Create a Context Pack from the AI MOC.

The final pack is optimized for AI assistants.

## 5. Ask

Upload or paste the Context Pack into:

- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini
- NotebookLM
- Claude Code

Then ask questions grounded in your notes.
